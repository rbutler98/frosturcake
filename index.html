About Us

Proud to be local, we're a studio focused on icing your own desserts. Our decorating studio is open and waiting for you! We hope you'll join us to experience the fun of decorating our cakes, cupcakes, and cookies using all the same tools the pros use in a fun, family-friendly environment that's great for any age! Hope to see you soon!

How it Works

If you've always wanted to try cake, cupcake, or cookie decorating but didn't know where to start, or didn't want the mess or expense, at Frost Your Cake Studio we provide all the tools, frosting, and decorations you need for an "unlimited decorating experience." Decorate cookies, cakes, or cupcakes however you desire. Use as many colors, frosting tips, and sprinkles as you need to bring your creation from idea to reality! All levels of experience and ages are welcome! Even if you've never decorated baked goods before, we're happy to help provide some inspiration or useful tips and tricks.
