How it Works

Do you love to decorate cookies, cakes and cupcakes? Or, maybe you’ve always wanted to try decorating, but didn’t know where to start, or didn’t want the mess or expense. At Frost Your Cake Studio we provide all the tools, frosting, and decorations you need for an “unlimited decorating experience”. Decorate cookies, cakes, or cupcakes however you desire. Use as many colors, frosting tips, and sprinkles as you need to bring your creation from idea to reality. All levels of experience and ages are welcome! Even if you’ve never decorated before, we’re happy to help provide some inspiration or useful tips and tricks.

Parties and Celebrations

We love celebrating! Bring your friends or family and celebrate special days with private parties. We’re the perfect spot for birthdays, bridal showers, baby showers, holidays, and special events. Reserve our private party room that holds up to 20 guests.
