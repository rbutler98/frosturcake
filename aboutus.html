Founded in 2025, Frost your cake studios is a local studio founded right here in Conway. Founded by Dustin and Julie Wear, we host a fun event schedule where individuals can come in and decorate their own baked goods! This studio has been a long dream coming for everyone involved in its making! We are ecstatic to be launching here soon! Can't wait to see y'all!

No reservation? No problem! We do both event based and walk-in decorating. Come in and choose between our wide variety of baked goods and desserts. We provide the treat and the icing, all you have to do is provide the art. No experience in using icing? Don't worry, all of our staff is more than experienced in techniques and tricks. Just ask any of us for help!
